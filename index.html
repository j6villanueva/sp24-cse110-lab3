Student Senate Regular Meeting Minutes

Friday, November 2nd, 2022

8:00 am - 11:00 am

Santa Barbara City College, Student Senate Room (CC 223)

721 Cliff Drive, Santa Barbara, CA 93109

NOTICE IS HEREBY GIVEN that the Student Senate of the Associated Student Government of Santa Barbara City College will hold a meeting on Friday, November 2nd, 2018 from 8:00 am–11:00 am. The meeting information is noted below. The Senate reserves the right to suspend the orders of the day if necessary to conduct business. All Senate meetings are held in locations that are wheelchair accessible. Other disability related accommodations will be provided to persons with disabilities upon request. Persons requesting such accommodations, or those who would like to attend this meeting by web or conference call, should notify Amy Collins, at collinsa@sbcc.edu, no less than two working days prior to the meeting. Meeting documents distributed before and at the meeting will be held at: (https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1MoCLtYk7hAioLKUrqXA9p_Ec1i7bHJZq?ogsrc=3 2)
Please contact the Senate Chair, President Joshua Villanueva, for any agenda related questions at Jtvillanueva@pipeline.sbcc.edu.

Pursuant to Government Code 54957.5: Supplemental materials distributed less than 72 hours before this meeting to a majority of the Senate will be made available for public inspection at this meeting and at the Office of Student Life by appointment during normal business hours. Materials prepared by Senate Members and distributed during the meeting are available for public inspection at the meeting or after the meeting if prepared by some other person. Documents will also be posted on our website. Documents related to closed session items or those that are exempt from disclosure as stated in the California Public Records Act will not be made available for public inspection.

Please note that additional materials, including attachments and reports, are available on the Associated Student Government’s website: www.asgsbcc.org
